Theoretical Training

Trainees get access to an online learning system including the necessary study materials. Each lesson (lecture) is provided in both text and video format. The duration of the training is 60 days. Every 1st of every month, a new group starts a course.

Price

The price of the course includes: Access to the online materials for the training period; Practical training with a teacher; The price is BGN 500 including VAT. There is an option to payoff, without increase in price, in 2 equal installments
